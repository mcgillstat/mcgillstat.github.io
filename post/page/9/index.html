    Long Nguyen · Oct 19, 2018

    Date: 2018-10-19 Time: 15:30-16:30 Location: BURN 1104 Abstract: Optimal transport plays an increasingly relevant and useful role in the theory and application of mixture model based clustering and inference.     Ashkan Ertefaie · Sep 28, 2018

Existing methods only focus on selecting the important variables for the decision-making process and fall short in providing inference for the selected model.
